## Make Your Immigration Process Easier

Immigration DNA testing differs from standard legal DNA tests. These cases must be:

- Initiated through an **AABB-accredited laboratory**
- Conducted under strict USCIS and U.S. Department of State compliance requirements

### Key Compliance Advantages

- **Petitioner-Initiated Testing** – ensures accuracy, legitimacy, and USCIS acceptance  
- **Strict Compliance Procedures** – every step aligns with federal immigration standards

## Steps to Begin Your DNA Test

### 1. Complete the Immigration Referral Form

Access the referral form and submit your case information.

**Important:**  
When asked *“Who referred you to our laboratory?”*, enter:

**J68 – Mile High DNA Testing**

This ensures your case is routed correctly through our office.

---

### 2. UFC Will Contact You With Next Steps

After submission, **UFC (our AABB-accredited lab partner)** will provide:

- Custom pricing based on your case
- Required forms and invoice  
- Payment options: credit cards or money orders (no checks)

---

### 3. DNA Sample Collection Arranged by UFC

**Inside the United States:**  
Appointments are scheduled at Mile High DNA Testing or another approved collection facility near you.

**Outside the United States:**  
DNA kits are sent directly to the appropriate **U.S. Embassy or designated international collection site** for your relative abroad.

---

### 4. Results Sent Directly to Immigration Authorities

Once testing is complete, **official DNA results are sent directly** to USCIS, passport agencies, or the requesting office. You will also receive a copy for your records.
